Ski Resort

CS340 - Bart Hough and Zack Wetekamm

Resource and management site for a Ski Resort conglomerate

Users can maintain Resorts, Lifts, Employees, Roles, and Schedule for their Ski Resort

Add, Update, and Remove

For any of the Ski Resort's entities, perform CRUD operations to alter the database.

The database uses Node.js to execute create, read, update, and delete functionalities.

Many Relationships

  • Resorts exists as a hierarchal entity
  • Lifts belong to specific Resorts
  • Employees can work a specific Lift and Role
